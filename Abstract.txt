Abstract for Final Project

A small, 2D, top-down style game. Will have multiple rooms that the player can progress through, 
collecting weapons and upgrades along the way. Weapon types will range from melee to ranged,
in the form of guns, magic spells, or other projectiles. The player will encounter enemies and traps,
and once the player dies they will be sent back to the start of the game. The game will be written with
PyGame.
